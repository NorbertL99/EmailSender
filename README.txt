///////////////About program/////////////////
/////////////////////////////////////////////

Program allow to send lots of emails just by one
click. Create your own templates for email list,
messages and accounts, follow history of your sends
manage users in your database.

/////////////////////////////////////////////
/////////////////Technology//////////////////
/////////////////////////////////////////////

Program was written in C# .NET framework in 
Visual Studio 2017 Community. Used SMTP protocal
and async programming, MD5 hashing, and DES data encrypt
As a database server XAMPP has been used

/////////////////////////////////////////////
//////////////////Version////////////////////
/////////////////////////////////////////////

The version is 1.0. Completed.

/////////////////////////////////////////////
////////////////How to applay////////////////
/////////////////////////////////////////////

First import database file into your database.
Than insert new user into database, REMEMBER TO 
HASH YOUR PASSWORD !!! Than go on your email 
account on gmail, to preper account. You have to
allow less trusted apps to connect to your account
More info:
https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/6010255?hl=pl

Than just run .exe of load files into VS and enjoy
